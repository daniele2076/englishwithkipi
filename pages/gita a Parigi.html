Last spring, Isabel went on a school trip to Paris. She traveled with her classmates and teachers by bus. They stayed in a youth hostel near the city center.

On the first day, they visited the Eiffel Tower. Isabel took photos and ate a croissant while looking at the view.

The group walked along the River Seine and saw boats and bridges. They also visited the Louvre Museum and saw the Mona Lisa.

One afternoon, they had a picnic in a park and played games. Isabel bought a small souvenir: a keychain shaped like the Eiffel Tower.

At night, they ate French food and talked about their favorite moments. Isabel said she wanted to come back to Paris one day.
