✈️ Leo’s Trip to Spain

Leo went on holiday to Spain with his family. They flew on a plane and landed in Barcelona. The city was full of colorful buildings, sunny streets, and delicious food.

Leo stayed in a small apartment near the beach. Every morning, he ate toast and fruit, then walked to the sea with his parents and sister.

He swam in the water, played football on the sand, and tried Spanish ice cream called “helado.”

One day, they visited a famous park called Park Güell. Leo saw mosaics, lizards made of tiles, and a big bench shaped like a snake.

At night, they watched a flamenco show with music and dancing. Leo clapped his hands and smiled. He loved his trip to Spain!
